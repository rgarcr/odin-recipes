Taco Pizza Recipe

Taco pizza

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2.25 ounce sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup Mexican-style shredded four-cheese blend
  • 12-inch prepared pizza crust
  • 1 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Cook and stir the ground beef, onion, and garlic together in a large skillet over medium-high heat until the beef is completely browned; drain fat from the skillet.
  2. Add sauce
  3. Add cheese
  4. Bake until crisp
